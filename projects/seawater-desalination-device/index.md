All students in Professor Begrambekov's lab participated in the desalination
project. For us it was mostly like a modern research-assistant role: we had our
own plasma work, but sometimes helped with the device development when the
project needed hands.

<!--more-->

The work ranged from vacuum condensation and boiling salted water to solar
collectors, glass-to-metal connections, and actual plasma deposition experiments
for the black coating on the collector pipes. It was a side job, but it taught
us a lot about practical product-engineering struggles: sealing, heating,
condensing, connecting unlike materials, and making a real prototype behave
outside the clean boundaries of a paper design.
